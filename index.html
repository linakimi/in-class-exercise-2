Mysteries of Peak

@DivineGamingChannel

This mystery in Peak was finally answered and it makes total sense.

When starting a match, one of the first things you may never notice on the shore is the conch shells that are everywhere.

But when you pick them up, you slowly figure out that they are useless.

You can't eat them after cooking it or use it as a projectile.

It simply fills up a slot and weighs a lot.

So why is it in the game?

Well... the creators of the game recently explaines why.

Aggro crab, one of the main developers, stated that it was an easter egg to another of their games.

Another Crabs Treasure, which was their most recently released game besides Peak.

The conch shell is of course a reference to Krill's shell at the beginning of the game.
